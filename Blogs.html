The Unique permutation
 Sometimes I feel that life is so much busy nowadays that even sitting idle can be considered as one of the most difficult task as we are always imagining that there is someone at this moment who is an inch ahead of us towards the finishing line . But should we consider life as some race ? I don't think so because life is there with us to learn and we are all the characters playing out part as Shakespeare said. Success and Failure , love and hatred , happiness and grief are relative terms as the condition of grief for someone is yet a dream for others as they are not even able to think of what grief is . Possibly the reason for this rat race in this modern world is a sudden understanding of inferiority and the sense that " I have nothing as compared to ...." . Do these really matter or effect to who we are . We always tend to be like other person who is likeable and being loved by everyone but shall we lose our own character just because it's the time for others to play their part .. One shall possess the uniqueness he has by birth till death and that must not be affected by other's perception of who we are or what is perfect for us to do . We should be happy of who we are , as scientifically the process of thinking of no two individuals can be same as we are a permutations of the genes which are no way the same for any two of us ..

Rejections always takes us from the luck to deserving class
I sometimes cannot find any reasons for my failure but I wonder that it is sometimes good to fail because then we try to uplift ourselves to the real situation where we can learn the things in a more nice manner .My mother always says whatever happens happens for the best , maybe it is true that life is trying to build some new twist for us which is better than what we expect .Since we can always see the present before us but can see the impact of our failures in the future. But let me mention this , if we keep working hard to achieve our dream and are not moved by failures , then it is for sure that we are going to achieve and see results what we haven't ever imagined. But sitting idle and saying that whatever happens happens for the best is just useless thing because as Shri Krishna says to Arjuna we should focus on work and not about the results , we should always be working towards our goal.

Sometimes you are bad being too good
Hello reader ,I am writing this blog after a lot of days and today I found that I should not be carrying the burden of thoughts that I am having right now not accusing of any individual for the same rather an observation made from everyday happenings , maybe a similar situation you too would face someday ,nobody can say ,though I hope that you wouldn't and that's why let me continue with the facts that I want to write down with all the experiences that I have .I know you might be busy but I hope that even in this busy schedule when you are bored of doing things which you have been doing for the last decade and not enough energy is left to pursue the things with the same zeal you started with. Sometimes life too denies the fact of you rather me being too good as it is habituated with the fallacy of faces carrying something in mind and wearing a smile which is truly never their own. I have experienced that the slightest of your achievements can make you out of the company of your mates where you used to play one of the leading roles someday and even you wouldn't even know when the stream of feelings of the person standing besides you changes its direction and you are still the same. Life is always such unpredictable and would always be .When you give special preferences to someone for the quanta of happiness you want to share ,it becomes a kind of betrayal in that person's eyes even if you are still thinking whether you have really done something .Sometimes someone expects you to be a perfect person but then philosophy says that you must be imperfect to learn something rather you would be out of purpose in this world the moment you get perfection inside your pocket .These are the plight of none other than the reader who is reading it and maybe you misjudged somebody someday ,you must know that we all live in a society which is always dragged by the inertia of power which is however a relative term and not carrying any specific meaning in reference to this para but at the end everyone is hoping to get some pennies to live happily in their own garret. Before judging somebody which we do every 2 seconds we must wear his shoes and try to know whether he had any other option or not and because of which "sometimes you are bad being too good " .
